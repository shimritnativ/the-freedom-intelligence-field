○ Power Reset Journey — 5 Status Categories

Every purchaser belongs in one of these categories. Your action depends entirely on where they are.

A

New Purchaser — Has Not Started

They bought but haven't taken the first step.

  • Send the welcome or starting message
  • Check if they need access guidance
  • Offer encouragement to begin
  • Help them find the first step
  • If several days have passed — send a restart invitation
B

Currently Completing the Power Reset

They are actively going through the experience.

  • Continue the sequence from their current day forward
  • Do NOT send old messages that no longer apply
  • If they're on Day 3 — continue from Day 3 and follow with what comes after
C

Started and Then Stopped

They began but dropped off at some point.

  • Identify the last day they completed
  • Send a personal follow-up based on where they stopped
  • Guide them back into the experience warmly
D

Completed the Power Reset

They finished all four days. Now they need the next step.

  • Send any post-Reset follow-up they missed
  • Invitation to a Connect Call (if applicable)
  • Invitation to Unlimited (if applicable)
  • Any relevant next step, limited code, or message that still applies
E

Purchased But Never Engaged

They bought but have never interacted or responded.

  • Send the restart message
  • Initiate a personal conversation
  • Goal: help them re-enter the experience — not leave them inactive

✎ Standard Note Format

Use this format every time you add a note in GoHighLevel. Consistency means anyone on the team can understand the contact history at a glance.

Date: [e.g. 13 July 2026]
Platform: [WhatsApp Business / GHL WhatsApp / Email / Instagram / Facebook]
Status: [e.g. Completed Day 3 / New Purchase / Stuck on Day 1 / Graduate]
Action taken: [what you did]
Message sent: [which message and what it contained]
Response: [Pending / Replied — summary / No reply]
Automation status: [Delivering / Not delivering / Unknown]
Next action: [exactly what needs to happen next]
Next follow-up date: [date]
Example Note
Date: 13 July 2026
Platform: WhatsApp Business
Status: Completed Day 3
Action taken: Manual follow-up sent — GHL WhatsApp not delivering (US number)
Message sent: Day 3 follow-up and invitation to reply with their experience
Response: Pending
Automation status: Not delivering
Next action: Follow up tomorrow — send post-Reset invitation if they completed Day 4
Next follow-up date: 14 July 2026
